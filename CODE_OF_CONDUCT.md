# Code of Conduct

Rondo is a small, maintainer-mediated project. Interactions happen mainly through
issues, and the bar is simple.

## The standard

Be respectful and constructive. Assume good faith. Keep discussion about the
work — bug reports, questions, and ideas — not the person.

Not welcome: harassment, personal attacks, demeaning or discriminatory comments,
or sustained disruption.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, discussions, commit comments, and
any official channel — and to anyone participating in them.

## Enforcement

The maintainer (Mark Hubers) may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate
this standard, and may block repeat offenders. Concerns can be raised privately
via the contact in `SECURITY.md`.

This is a deliberately short code of conduct; for the spirit behind it, the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) is a good reference.
