## What's LEFT ‚Äî ranked, top 10

### 1. MED-HIGH ‚Äî Idempotency **in-memory `_cache` is still unbounded** on the long-lived MCP server
The campaign closed every *other* growth surface ‚Äî JSONL compaction, `sweep_stale_key_locks`, ref-counted `_key_locks` (RONDO-369) ‚Äî but the in-memory dict is the one left open. `_cache` (`idempotency.py:59`) only evicts a key when *that same key* is re-looked-up after TTL (`idempotency.py:451-456`); `cache_result` (`:480`) and the file‚Üímemory promote (`:468`) add an entry per unique prompt, and nothing ever sweeps expired-but-unread entries. A server seeing unique prompts (every distinct Cursor prompt is a new SHA-256 key) grows one tuple **holding the full result payload** forever. This is the in-memory twin of exactly the disk/lock leaks RONDO-369/396 just fixed. NEW.
